Adding Genre and Copyright Information to MEI Metadata

When adding genre, I added it to the notesStmt section under the work section. I decided to also add period and style, because these are further subclassifications especially for the classical genre of music that my portfolio is focused on. Next, I decided to use CC0 1.0 because the song itself is from 1894, so it's public domain. I didn't add any originality to the work; I simply encoded the existing data into a more easily-readable file. Users are allowed to share for any use without permission or requirement to attribute me (even though it can still be preferred).
